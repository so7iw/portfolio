About me

My name is Milos Stojanovski, I am a student of ICT College in Belgrade, Serbia. Programming, coding and digital design have always been a center of my interest, thus I always strived to excell in theese areas, with my primary goal being set on backend developing. I am also working full time at Euronet World Wide, as Central Level 1 System Analyst.
But, however my interests about profession may appear formal, I always had hobbies, of which photography is the only one I succeeded at keeping in my life. And this portfolio is just the place to share my work with you. To let you see what I see when I look through the window. To see the beauty within.
